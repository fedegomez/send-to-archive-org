Automatically send the public contents of your website to Archive.org to have always available a history with backups of them.

== Description ==

**Send to Archive.org** is an innovative WordPress plugin designed to facilitate the digital preservation of your web content. With this plugin, you can automatically send your posts, pages, and custom post types (CPTs) to Archive.org, one of the world's largest digital libraries, renowned for its extensive web archiving project, the Wayback Machine.

#### Key Features

- **Automatic Submission to Archive.org**: Automatically save your publications to Archive.org every time you publish or update content on your WordPress site.
- **Support for Various Content Types**: Compatible with posts, pages, and all custom post types that are public and accessible on the front-end.
- **Deep Integration with WordPress**: Designed to seamlessly integrate with the WordPress admin interface, providing a consistent and familiar user experience.
- **Bulk Submission Functions**: Send content to Archive.org in bulk, allowing you to archive multiple posts or pages simultaneously with a single click.
- **Email Notifications**: Option to receive email notifications each time content is sent to Archive.org, keeping you informed about the status of your submissions.
- **Custom Column in Post Listings**: Display Archive.org information, such as the date of the last submission, directly in your admin panel's post listings.
- **Flexible Configuration**: Customize the settings according to your needs, choosing which content types to include and whether you want to receive email notifications.

#### Why Use Send to Archive.org?

1. **Content Preservation**: Ensure that your digital content is safe and accessible for future generations, effectively preserving your website's history.
2. **Ease of Use**: Simple setup and an automated submission process make preserving your content as easy as clicking a button.
3. **Flexibility**: Whatever your content type, from blogs and informational articles to portfolios and project pages, Send to Archive.org is equipped to handle it.
4. **Selective Archiving**: Choose exactly which types of content you want to archive, providing you with control over what is preserved.
5. **Efficiency and Convenience**: The plugin's bulk action feature saves time and effort, allowing for quick and easy archiving of multiple items at once.
6. **Peace of Mind**: With Send to Archive.org, rest assured that your content is preserved on a reliable and globally accessible platform.

Enhance your website's longevity and historical significance with Send to Archive.org, your one-stop solution for easy and efficient web content archiving.

== Installation ==

Installing **Send to Archive.org** is straightforward and follows the standard WordPress plugin installation process. Here's how you can get it up and running on your WordPress site:

1. **Upload via WordPress Dashboard**
   1. Log in to your WordPress dashboard.
   2. Navigate to **Plugins > Add New**.
   3. Click the **Upload Plugin** button at the top of the page.
   4. Select the ZIP file of the **Send to Archive.org** plugin from your local computer and click **Install Now**.
   5. Once the upload is complete, click **Activate Plugin** to activate Send to Archive.org on your WordPress site.

2. **Install via WordPress Plugin Directory**
   1. From your WordPress dashboard, navigate to **Plugins > Add New**.
   2. In the search box, type **Send to Archive.org** and press Enter.
   3. Once you find the plugin, click **Install Now**.
   4. After installation, click **Activate** to start using the plugin.

3. **Manual Installation via FTP**
   1. Download the ZIP file of the Send to Archive.org plugin to your computer.
   2. Unzip the file. This will create a new folder with the plugin files.
   3. Using an FTP client, connect to your website server.
   4. Upload the plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory on your web server.
   5. Once uploaded, go to your WordPress dashboard, navigate to **Plugins > Installed Plugins**.
   6. Locate **Send to Archive.org** and click **Activate**.

#### Post-Installation Setup

After activation, you can configure the plugin to suit your needs:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Send to Archive.org** in your WordPress dashboard.
2. Here, you can select the post types you want to archive, set up email notifications, and configure other settings.
3. Once configured, your selected content will be automatically archived according to your settings.
